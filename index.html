How long does it take to learn how to Code?

It depends on consistency. Some people start building projects in 3-6 months, while mastery takes longer.

Do i need a computer science to get into tech?

No. A degree can help, but many successful developers are self-taught. Skills and projects matter most.

What projects should I build as a beginner?

Start small (portfolio site, to-do list, landing page), but make sure you aim at solving a problem. The world seeks problem solvers.

Can i get a tech job without experience?

Yes-- But you'll need to showcase your work. Build projects, contribute to open source, and share your journey online.

What languages should I learn first?

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are the foundation of web devlopment. After that, you can branch into frameworks and backend

Is it too late to start learning how to code?

Not at all. People start at different age and still build successful careers.

How can I stay motivated while learning?

Break your goals into small wins, Join commmunities, and build projects that matters to you.
